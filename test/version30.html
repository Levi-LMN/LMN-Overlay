Process

From setup to
going live in minutes.

STEP 01
Create Your Account
Sign up with email or Google. Your 7-day free trial starts immediately — no credit card required. Access the full platform from day one.
STEP 02
Configure Your Overlays
Customise text, colours, ticker content, and position for each event type. Use the OCR tool to scan printed programmes and auto-fill tickers.
STEP 03
Add to OBS or vMix
Copy the display URL for your event type and add it as a Browser Source in OBS Studio or vMix. The overlay renders transparently over your video.
STEP 04
Go Live & Control in Real Time
During the event, toggle overlays on/off, update text, and control tickers live from any device — phone, tablet, or laptop — from the control panel.
